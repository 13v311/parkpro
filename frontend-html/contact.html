About the Creator

Hi! My name is Levell Kensey and I started my first year of college in August of 2024! I'm a computer science major and I'll tell you all about why I decided to make this website to help students who drive to UIC! As a commuter student myself, I found it hard to choose a parking lot during the summer before my first year of college that I would be parking in for the entire semester(s). I had no idea where any of those lots were (except Halsted Street Parking Structure, but it was full), but I needed to pick a parking assignment fast, so I went with Lot 6. While this lot was decent for my first semester, it was incredibly far away from my morning class second semester which was in BSB. This ultimately inspired me to create this tool to help students have more knowledge about UIC's on-campus lots, as well as how close their classes are to them. That way, even if you are unsure where that lot is, you'll quickly have access to learn info about it. Plus, I hope to soon have tips from other users such as yourself that can further help students!

You can also submit feedback back on the homepage!
