Student-Defined Priority Parking

ParkPro helps you choose a parking lot based on how you rank your classes according to start time sensitivity--especially useful for morning/evening classes and during inclement weather conditions.

Advantages

- Gives you more control over your own schedule

- Ideal for minimizing effort to get to important or time-sensitive classes

Automated Building Proximity Parking

ParkPro helps you find the closest parking lot to each class building based on the number of times it appears as the closest. Exclude unwanted lots for best results.

Advantages

- Great for schedules that jump between distant buildings

- Minimizes walking distance to any class
