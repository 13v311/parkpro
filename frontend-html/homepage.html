How ParkPro Helps You

Choose From Two Parking Strategies

ParkPro offers two strategies to recommend lots to you. One allows you to rank your classes in terms of how important it is to get to each on time, while the other has ParkPro give its own rankings based on closeness to all parking lots.

Tell Us Your Preferences

You'll be able to give us your full class schedule (either manually or by uploading an ICS file which can be downloaded from XE registration), then you can also exclude any lots you don't want in your results.

View Results and Go From There

You'll receive recommended parking lots as well as an interactive map that allows you to visually see where all of your classes and recommended lots are. From there, you can decide what you'd like to do (i.e., apply for a pass, park there for the day, etc.)!
